August 2025 - C175 to Kenora, ON (maybe Steinbach, MB) w/ Extra Ontario Stops

Currently estimated dates:
Depart -> Thursday/Friday August 14/15 2025 Return -> Sunday/Monday August 17/18 2025 Things remaining to be done absolutely before trip:
- Test aux fuel system
- Gather more tiedown options (at least 2?)
- Test filing flight plans with FPG
- Weigh major items
- Buy oil
- Call about fuel/landing fees/parking fees
- Download suppplementary docs (AIM, manuals, etc.)

North route

CYRP -> 4605N07718W -> CMA2 -> CYYB -> CYXR -> CNE4 -> CYYU -> CYHF -> CYGQ -> CYYW* -> CYXL -> CYQK
OTTAWA (CARP) -> Lat/Long -> Mattawa -> North Bay -> EARLTON -> Iroquois Falls -> Kapuskasing -> HEARST -> GERALDTON -> Armstrong* -> Sioux Lookout -> KENORA

* = No 100LL Fuel
CAPS = Fuel Stop
Bold = Stop

852 NM * 2

Good places to stay overnight, in order of appearance:
- Earlton (Uncontrolled, 42 minute walk to town, food, cheap fuel)
- Kapuskasing (Class E, 51 min walk from town, food, cheap fuel)
- Hearst (Uncontrolled, 50 min walk from town, food, cheap fuel)
- Geraldton (Class E, not close to town, but maybe camp?)
- Armstrong (Uncontrolled, not close to town, but maybe camp?, no fuel)

Likely fuel stops (not necessarily stay overnight):
- CYXR
- CYYU or CYHF
- CYGQ (needed before trip to Armstrong/Sioux Lookout)
- CYXL, if necessary, before final hop to Kenora

Rough one day ideal scenario legs plan:
Day 1:
Leg 1: CYRP - Custom Point - CMA2 - CYXR (228 NM, ~2.1 hrs)
Leg 2: CYXR - CNE4 - CYYU - CYHF (202 NM, ~1.8 hrs)
Leg 3: CYHF - CYQG (126 NM, ~1.2 hrs)
Leg 4: CYQG - CYYW - CYXL - CYQK (296 NM, ~2.7 hrs)

Assuming 45 minutes per fuel break x3: 2.3 hrs Total time: 7.9 hrs + 2.3 hrs = 10.2 hrs

Earliest departure: Sunrise 6 AM Local (10 AM UTC) + 10.2 hrs = 3:15 PM Local
Latest departure: 11:45 AM Local = Sunset 9 PM Local (2 AM UTC) - 10.2 hrs

Rough two day ideal scenario legs plans:

Day 1:
Leg 1: CYRP - Custom Point - CMA2 (~1.5 hrs)
Leg 2: CMA2 - (CYYB) - CYXR (~0.75 hrs)
- FUEL AT CYXR (confirm any landing fees?)
Leg 3: CYXR - CYTS - CYYU (~1.3 hrs)
- STAY IN CYYU OR CYHF (confirm any lading and parking fees?)
Leg 3/4: CYYU - CYHF (confirm any lading and parking fees?) (~0.5 hrs)
- STAY IN CYYU OR CYHF

Day 2:
Leg 1: CYYU/CYHF - CYGQ
- FUEL AT CYQG (confirm any landing fees?)
- LOOK AROUND QUICKLY
Leg 2: CYGQ - CYYW - CYXL
- FUEL AT CYXL IF NO LANDING FEES (confirm any landing fees?)
- IF NO LANDING FEE AND PARKING FEE, VISIT TOWN
Leg 3: CYXL - CYQK
STAY IN KENORA

2026? - C175 to Arviat, NU?

The trip would be completed weather dependent on the weeks between Aug 4th 2025 to Sept 8th 2025.
It is not necessary to fly all legs but instead just try to push as far as safely possible and with enough time to return safely.

Leg 1
CYRP -> CYYB -> CYTS (310 NM, ~ 2.8 hrs)
Altitudes: 2400 ft min, 8500 ft max
Airspace to consider: Ottawa Class C, CYR 540, Algonquin MOA, North Bay Class E, CYA 521
Leg 2
CYTS -> CYGQ -> CYPL (390 NM, ~ 3.5 hrs)
Altitudes: 2100 ft min, 8500 ft max
Airspace to consider: Geraldton Class E
Leg 3
CYPL -> CJT3 -> CYGX (353 NM, ~ 3.2 hrs)
Altitudes: 2000 ft min
Airspace to consider: Knee Lake Class G
Leg 4
CYGX -> CYYQ -> CYEK (299 NM, ~ 2.7 hrs)
Altitudes: 1300 ft min
Airspace to consider: Churchill Class E

Leg 5
CYEK -> CYYQ -> CYGX (299 NM, ~ 2.7 hrs)
Altitudes: 1300 ft min
Leg 6
CYGX -> CJT3 -> CYPL (353 NM, ~ 3.2 hrs)
Altitudes: 2000 ft min
Leg 7
CYPL -> CYGQ -> CYTS (390 NM, ~ 3.5 hrs)
Altitudes: 2100 ft min, 9500 ft max
Leg 8
CYTS -> CYYB -> CYRP (310 NM, ~ 2.8 hrs)
Altitudes: 2400 ft min, 9500 ft max

Total baseline flight time: 24.4 hrs, at $225/hr then ~$5500 (just aicraft operating costs). Split between two pilots: $2750

Possible add on visits, time and wind/performance permiting:
- Stop in CYYQ, Churchill
- Stop in CYGQ, Geraldton
- Deviation to Kenora, CYQK?
