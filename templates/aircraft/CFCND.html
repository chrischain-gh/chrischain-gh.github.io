  1. Fuel Selector – Both

  4. Mixture – Rich

  7. Throttle – Open 1/4"
  8. Beacon Light – On
  9. Master Switch – On
  10. Prop Area – Clear
  11. Ignition Switch – Both

  19. Lights – As Required
TAXI
  1. Brakes – Check
  2. Flight Controls – Set for Wind
RUNUP
  1. Brakes – Set
  2. Cabin Doors – Closed
  3. Flight Controls – Free & Correct
  4. Flight Instruments – Check/Set
  5. Mixture – Rich, or Lean & Smooth
  6. Throttle – 1700 RPM
  7. Oil Pressure – Check
  8. Suction Gauge – Green
  9. Ammeter – Charging
  10. Carb Heat – Check for RPM Drop
  11. Mags – Max 175 RPM Drop
  12. Throttle – 1000 RPM
  13. Oil Temp – >100
  14. Radios/Avionics – Set
  15. Departure Plan – Review
  16. Flaps – 0° or 20° Short/Soft Field
Placceholder
  1. Throttle – At least 50% Power
  2. Mixture – Lean until Engine Roughness Begins
  3. Time – 1-2 Minutes
  4. Mixture – Halfway Back to Full Rich
  5. Ignition Switch – Check L & R
  6. Repeat if Needed, Note Which Ignition Side is Rough
  7. Throttle – Idle
  8. Proceed to Takeoff Checklist or Return to Hangar
TAKEOFF
  1. Unmarked V Speeds in MPH:
    1. V70
    2. Vx 61 with 20° Flaps
    3. Vy 84
    4. V123
    5. Best Glide 70
  2. Trim – Set for Takeoff
  3. Mixture – Rich or Lean ≤4,000'
  4. Landing Lights – On
  5. Fuel Pump – On
  6. Time – Note
CLIMB
  1. Flaps – Up above Obstacles
  2. Climb Above 1,000' AGL:
    1. Airspeed – 90 MPH
    2. Power – Full
    3. Fuel Pump – Off
    4. Mixture – Lean ≤4,000'/CHT <400°F
  3. Cruise:
    1. Pitch – Level
    2. RPM – 2350-2700 RPM
    3. Trim – Set
    4. Mixture – Lean, CHTs <400°F
    5. Lights – As Required
    6. Engine Instruments – Check/Monitor
  4. Descent:
    1. Destination Weather – Check
    2. Throttle – As Required
  5. Carb Heat – On
  6. Prop – Check RPM not in Red
  7. Mixture – Enrich as Required
  8. Landing Lights – On
  9. Before Landing:
    1. Seat Belts – On
    2. Fuel Selector – Both
    3. Mixture – Rich
    4. Fuel Pump – On
    5. Throttle – As Required
    6. Carb Heat – On
  10. Approach Speeds at Max Gross Weight:
    1. Flaps 0-20°: 70-80 MPH
    2. Flaps 30-40°: 63-70 MPH
  11. Go-Around:
    1. Throttle – Smoothly Full Forward
    2. Carb Heat – Cold
    3. Flaps – Retract Slowly with Airspeed Increase
  12. After Landing:
    1. Flaps – Up
    2. Carb Heat – Cold
    3. Mixture – Ground Lean
    4. Fuel Pump – Off
    5. Lights – As Required
    6. Radios – Set
    7. Transponder – Check
